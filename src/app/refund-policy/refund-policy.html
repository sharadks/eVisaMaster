Refund and cancellation

Refund and cancellation of the e-Visa service fees:- indiaonlinevisas.org.in gives it's users a chance to apply for refund /cancellation in case the applicants want to discontinue with our services. The applicants can apply for refund within 30 Minutes of submission of application subject to the following condition:-
A) No refund/cancellation in case the travel is within 24-48 hours.
B) If we received your request for cancellation/refund within 30 Minutes after the submission of application, we will refund the payment after deduction of 50% towards the cancellation charges.
C) If you wish to request a refund, email us at info@indiaonlinevisas.org.in indicating the following
Your reason for the request.
Your full names (as appears in your passport).
Your unique transaction ID.
The email used to make the registration on this website.
E) All refund requests will be processed within 72 hours.
